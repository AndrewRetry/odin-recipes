Chocolate Truffles

Ingredients

Truffles

  • 285g dark chocolate (chocolate chips/chocolate block)
  • 2 tbsp (30g) unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup (125ml) cream (full fat)

Coating (Optional)

  • 1/2 cup dessicated coconut
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup crushed/ground peanuts
  • 1/3 cup sprinkles or similar
  • 1/4 cup icing sugar/powdered sugar
